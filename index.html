Location

Robert & Mary Capp, hosts and grandparents of the groom, built their beautiful family lake house in Pinnacle Lake Estates, a private, gated, recreational development that has grown around a 130 acre reservoir. The lake got its name from the large rock formation that juts up over 100 feet from the bottom of the lake. Pinnacle Lake Rock Shelter Site is officially listed on the National Register of Historic Places.
